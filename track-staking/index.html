How to Track Crypto Staking Properly

Why Staking Breaks Most Trackers

  • Staking rewards treated as buys
  • Spot and staked mixed together
  • Distorted PnL calculations

Correct Structure

  1. Track spot balance separately.
  2. Track staking balance separately.
  3. Sum both into total holdings.
  4. Keep cost basis logic intact.
