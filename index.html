Learn: crypto portfolio tracking (simple & reliable)

If you’ve tried multiple apps and still feel like your numbers don’t match — this section explains the “why” and how to fix it with a transparent spreadsheet workflow. For a full tool comparison, read the Best Crypto Portfolio Tracker guide.

What is a crypto portfolio tracker?

A portfolio tracker is a tool that aggregates your holdings, cost basis and performance across assets. The key is correctness: a tracker must treat deposits, buys, sells and fees consistently.

Why Google Sheets instead of an app?

Sheets gives you ownership and auditability. You can see the inputs, formulas and logic, and you keep access even if a SaaS changes pricing or removes features.

No subscription vs subscription

Subscription apps often optimize for recurring revenue. A one-time template is a tool you own — ideal if you value stability and long-term control.

Common tracking mistakes (and fixes)

  • Wrong cost basis → use consistent logic (FIFO / average) and include fees.
  • Prices missing → use auto price updater + manual fallback only when needed.
  • Inputs mixed with formulas → separate “edit” sheets from “auto” calculations.
  • Staking confusion → track staking separately, then sum into totals.
